About AuStudyFinder

📊

Data Source

Course data is sourced from the CRICOS (Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students) — the official Australian Government registry. Last updated: March 2, 2026.

🔍

How to Search

Type any course name, institution name, or field of study in the search bar. Use filters to narrow by course level, state, or budget. Results show active courses by default.

🎓

Scholarships

The Scholarships tab lists major Australian and international scholarships not included in the CRICOS database. Each entry includes eligibility, value, and official links.

💡

Tips

  • CRICOS codes starting with 0 are often universities
  • Check Work Component to find courses with practical training
  • Dual Qualification courses lead to two qualifications simultaneously
  • Costs shown are estimated total course cost for international students
